Our Exhibitions

Explore our participation in trade shows, product launches, and industry expos where we showcase our latest innovations

Greater Noida • August 2025

Fan Expo Conclave 2025

We participated in the Fan Expo Conclave 2025 at IEML, Greater Noida, showcasing our comprehensive range of smart control solutions. Our exhibition featured our premium remotes for fans, TVs, and ACs, along with demonstrations of our in-house production capabilities including BLDC motor controllers and advanced PCB manufacturing. The event provided an excellent platform to connect with dealers, distributors, architects, interior designers, and industry professionals from the fan and home automation sectors.

Bangkok • November 2025

Terminal & Tap-on-Phone Vendor Workshop

We participated in Mastercard's vendor workshop focused on contactless payment technologies and type approval processes for the Asia Pacific region. The event brought together terminal OEMs and tap-on-phone vendors to discuss Mastercard mandates, deployment strategies, and certification pathways.

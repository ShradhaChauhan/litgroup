About LIT Group

Leading the industry with innovation and excellence since 1995

LIT (Let's Innovate Together) is a leading electronics manufacturer in India , delivering reliable and scalable manufacturing solutions to global brands across consumer and industrial segments. Established in 1995, we bring over 30 years of electronics manufacturing experience, combining engineering expertise, robust infrastructure, and a deep understanding of OEM and ODM requirements.

As a trusted EMS company in India, we support our partners across the entire product lifecycle, from design and PCB assembly to high volume production and final box build. Our focus remains on precision, consistency, and long term value creation for every customer we serve.

1995 - The Beginning

LIT India ws incorporated, marking the beginning of our journey toward innovation and excellence. This marked the foundation of our commitment to quality manufacturing and innovation. Our initial focus was on precision tooling and plastic components, setting the stage for our future growth in the electronics industry.

With just a handful of employees and a vision to deliver excellence, we established our first manufacturing facility in Noida.

2005 - Entering Consumer Electronics

With a decade of manufacturing expertise, we ventured into consumer electronics by starting remote control manufacturing. This strategic move allowed us to leverage our precision manufacturing capabilities in the growing electronics market.

Our first remote control production line was established with a capacity of 100,000 units per month.

2012 - Diversification

Building on our success in remote controls, we expanded our product portfolio to include various electronic products, diversifying its offering and strengthening its position in the ODM & OEM sector. This diversification helped us establish a stronger presence in the electronics manufacturing sector.

We invested in advanced SMT lines and expanded our manufacturing facilities to accommodate new product categories.

2014 - Global Innovation

We established our R&D Centre in China to enhance our innovation capabilities and stay at the forefront of technology. This strategic move strengthened our ability to develop cutting-edge solutions for our clients.

The R&D center became our innovation hub, focusing on next-generation remote control technologies including RF, IR, and early voice-control prototypes.

2018 - Market Leadership

Achieved the position of India's leading remote control supplier, a testament to our commitment to quality and innovation. This milestone reflected our growing influence in the electronics manufacturing industry.

By 2018, our production capacity had expanded to over 1.5 million remote controls per month.

2019 - Strategic Partnerships

Successfully onboarded major brands including Voltas, LG, Samsung, and Jio, expanding our client base and strengthening our position as a trusted manufacturing partner for leading companies.

These partnerships enabled us to implement dedicated production lines with customized quality control measures for each brand.

2020 - Product Expansion

Diversified our product range by entering the adapters and chargers market, demonstrating our ability to adapt and grow in response to market demands and technological advancements.

Despite challenges, we successfully launched our power solutions division with products certified to international safety standards.

2021 - Innovation Milestone

Started the production of RF & Bluetooth voice control remotes, showcasing our commitment to innovation and staying ahead of technological trends in the remote control industry.

These smart remotes are engineered for efficiency and convenience, offering significantly lower power consumption, extended operational range, and seamless compatibility with leading voice assistants such as Alexa and Google Assistant.

2023 - New Horizons

Entered the payment solutions market by manufacturing soundboxes for PAYTM, marking another significant milestone in our journey of growth and diversification.

This strategic expansion into fintech hardware opened doors to the broader financial technology sector, positioning LIT Group for continued growth.

2025 - Advanced Manufacturing

Established advanced SMT (Surface Mount Technology) manufacturing setup, significantly enhancing our production capabilities and precision in electronic component assembly.

This investment in cutting-edge SMT technology enables us to deliver higher quality products with improved efficiency, positioning LIT Group at the forefront of modern electronics manufacturing.

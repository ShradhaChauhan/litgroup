About LIT Group

LIT (Let's Innovate Together) is a trusted remote manufacturer and electronics OEM/ODM partner, driven by innovation, quality, and customer-centric values. Established in 1995, LIT has grown from a small tool and injection molding workshop into a leading OEM and ODM manufacturer, proudly serving some of the biggest global brands across sectors.
